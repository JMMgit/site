Guidelines and Tips

Important Tips

Teacher must add student using smart phone.

There is no way to add friend in kakao with desktop software.

Teacher without grade will not be shown in the list of Recommended teacher.

But they will be shown if the option "Show all teachers" is selected.

Graded Teacher's must have Promotion Schedule

  • 1 schedule with 10mins duration and 500points

  • 1 schedule with 20mins duration and 1000points

Teacher's Guideline

  • Nickname should be less than or equal to 8 letters.

  • Profile image is best fit on 100px of width and 120px of height.

Bad Student's Guideline

  • If Student don't behave, talk to them in a nice way.

  • If Student doing other things, tell them to focus on your class in a nice way

  • If Student doing naughty things, tell them that we don't entertain those kind of activity.

  • If Student doing that is not right, take a screen shot and end the class. Inform the admin immediately by providing proper evidence.
