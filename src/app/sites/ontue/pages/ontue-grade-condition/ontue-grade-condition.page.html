TEACHER GRADE

GRADE 1

Having only good english teaching skill does not help to earn grade.

A Good teacher must have

  • Good Environment/Work Place. ( No unwanted sounds, No people at the back, etc... )
  • Good Devices. ( PC/Laptop, Headset with mic)
  • Good Internet. ( Reasonable Connection )
  • Good Profile Picture.
  • Good Video Introduction.
  • Prepared and Always ready to teach.
  • Finishing the evaluation on time.
  • Following our promotion schedule.
  • Following our guidelines.
  • Following our policy.
  • Always present in class.
  • Always records their class.

GRADE 2

Communication comes first then the teaching comes next. No matter how good you teach, but you failed to communicate with the student. It will not work.

A Good teacher must have

  • Passed all criteria from grade 1.
  • Capable of winning student's heart.
  • Good Communication Skills.
  • Good Teaching Skills.

GRADE 3

Consistent Reservation. Good Performance, Good Attitude, and Professionalism.

A Good teacher who is

  • Passed all criteria from grade 2.
  • Popular to students.
  • Most Requested teacher.
  • Giving Importance to student growth.
  • Always winning the student's heart.
